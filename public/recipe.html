Instructions

  • step 1

    Measure 60g of oats into a container along with 200ml of milk and sweeten it with as much jaggery as you want but be weary of it ;) and to finish it of add some cinnamon and coffee powder to it

  • step 2

    heat it until a very thick consistency

  • step 3

    now to top it off add some raisins and flaxseeds on top of it and mix it well

  • step 4

    now this is an additional step but this will enrich the dish to another level, what you gotta do is let the dish sit there for 5 minutes, what this will do is create a thick layer on top which will give you that crusty taste!
